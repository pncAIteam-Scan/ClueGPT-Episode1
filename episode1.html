Who Stole the Silver Bell?

Festival Music
Detective ambience
Volume

Rain had not touched Bellacqua in weeks, yet the stones along the old harbor glistened as if the lake had crept ashore.

Detective Cluewell arrived by boat just after dusk, their black coat stirring in the warm wind. Above them, the town climbed the hillside in crooked layers: shuttered windows, iron balconies, lemon trees, and one chapel tower that should have been ringing.

But the bell never sounded.

Detective Cluewell

The annual Festival of Saint Aurelia cannot begin without the Saint's Silver Bell. At midnight, the priest pulled the velvet cord. No sound came.

The bell was gone.

Detective Cluewell looked across the water and said, “Someone here has mistaken silence for innocence.”
